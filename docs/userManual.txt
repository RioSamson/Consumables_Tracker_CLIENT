How to do Operations

Note* the information comes from the server, so server must be running

1:
Once the program is running, to list all items, click on the "All" button at the top of the screen.
Can scroll to see all items.

2:
To add a new Item to the program, click the "Add Item" button at the bottom of the screen.
A pop up dialog should open. Enter in all the required fields correctly.
Once done, click "Create" button at the bottom.
The main page should show the new item in all.
To cancel from the dialog, press the "Cancel" button at the bottom of the dialog screen.

3:
To remove an item, once in the main screen, click on "All" button at the top of the screen.
From the list of foods, click the "Remove" button to the right of the item to remove it.

4:
To list all expired items, once at the main screen,
click on the "Expired" button at the top of the screen.
Can scroll to see all items.

5:
To list all non expired items, once at the main screen,
click on the "Not Expired" button at the top of the screen.
Can scroll to see all items.

6:
To list all items expiring in 7 days, once at the main screen,
click on the "Expiring in 7 days" button at the top of the screen.
Can scroll to see all items.

7:
To Exit, once at the main screen, click the "X" button in the top right
corner of the window.
